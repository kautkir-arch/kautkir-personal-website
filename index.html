Studio portrait

Between the stage and the screen.

Utkirbek Kakhorov is an Uzbek stage and cinema actor who performs at the Art House Hamsa experimental theatre in Tashkent. He is also recognised as a screenwriter for his work in television.

Acting in Uzbek, Russian, English and Turkish, he brings a broad cultural perspective to his roles — a range shaped by training at the Tashkent Film Academy and ongoing work across drama and screen.
